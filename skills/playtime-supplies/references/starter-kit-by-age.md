# Starter kit by age band

Tiered recommendations: starter (~$50), stocked (~$150), well-stocked (~$300+).

For each line, pull the search keyword from `by-category.md` to build the affiliate URL.

---

## 0-2 years (infant / young toddler)

### Starter (~$30-50)
- Sensory bin (large flat tub)
- Triangle / chunky crayons
- Washable finger paint
- Kid-sized art smock
- Bubbles + wand
- Sidewalk chalk

### Stocked (+$50)
- Play-Doh classic pack
- Pom-poms (assorted)
- Stickers (bulk assorted, big size)
- Newsprint pad (big paper)
- Painter's tape (sensory paths)

### Well-stocked (+$100)
- Magnetic tiles (40-piece starter)
- Kinetic sand (assorted colors)
- A wooden treasure basket (or use any basket from home)

## Universal: always-on-hand pantry

These are dirt cheap or already in the kitchen — flag in any list:

- Baking soda
- Vinegar (white)
- Salt
- Sugar
- Cornstarch
- Vegetable oil
- Food coloring (worth buying once)
- Flour
- Mason jars or clear cups (for observation)
- Cotton balls
- Paper plates / cups
- Aluminum foil
- Plastic wrap
- Ice cube trays
- Recycled cardboard / TP rolls / egg cartons (FREE — actively save these)

## Format for shopping-list output

When generating a personalized shopping list:

1. **Group by tier** — what to buy now vs. nice-to-add later
2. **Include free reuses** at the top — "actively save: TP rolls, egg cartons, cardboard boxes" (these unlock 30% of crafts)
3. **Note what they already have** — pulled from `materials` in profile.json — and skip duplicate recommendations
4. **Estimate total** — rough — use $ ranges, not exact prices
5. **Add the FTC disclosure footer**
